Privacy Policy

Your Privacy is Absolute.

The NoRules? app is built with a zero-data collection philosophy. We believe your board game nights are your own business.

  • No Accounts: You do not need to register, log in, or provide an email address to use any features.
  • No Tracking: We do not track your search history, favorite lists, or location data. Everything stays stored completely offline on your own device.
  • No Third Parties: Because we collect zero data, nothing is ever shared, sold, or distributed to third-party services or advertisers.
